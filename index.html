The curator

About Roos

I'm Roos — and I've always been drawn to things made by hand. There's something about a piece of jewelry that someone crafted with care, that carries the memory of a tradition passed down through generations, that feels completely different from anything mass-produced.

My connection to India runs deep. I fell in love with the country's craft traditions — the intricate goldwork, the bold bangles, the necklaces that somehow feel ancient and alive at the same time. Gulab is my way of bringing those pieces here to the Netherlands, and sharing something I genuinely love.

Every piece I carry is one I'd wear myself. I'm selective, I'm honest, and I care about getting it to you safely. This isn't a shop — it's a curation.

— Roos
